About Me

Hello!

My name is Mackenzie Peters. I am a BA Design Studies student at the University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee and I will be graduating in May 2020.

Currently, I work as a technical recruiter and experiential marketing intern for Skills Pipeline, a technical recruiting firm based out of Mequon, WI. In my free time, I work as a freelance graphic and web designer for clients throughout the United States. I love working and staying busy by creating compelling designs and well-functioning websites.

I am fueled by my passion to continue learning and growing. I've been a hard worker since birth, so I am never satisfied with a job until it's completed to perfection. If this means that I need to pick up new skills in order to get it done and do it right, then so be it!

In addition to my freelance work, I am currently looking for full-time positions in web design that will begin once I've graduated this may and I'm always open to hearing from you! Feel free to reach out to me using the contact page, or connect with me on LinkedIn!
